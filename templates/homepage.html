Ready to find your Pup some playmates?

Welcome to PuppyPlaymates, your dogs own playmate finding social

What is PuppyPlaymates

Here at PuppyPlaymates we wanted to create a way for dogs to find puppy love

Forget your conventional forms of social media and dating websites. We have created a way for dogs of all ages to meet online, to let other owners know when and where they are next walking their dog, to add photos and to comment on each others profiles.

You can set your dogs profile to show whether you are looking for plantonic playmates or looking for a soulmate for your pup!

Ready to get started?

Create an account and start your pups journey to a life full of playmates
  • Click the register button and set your username (This is for you as the owner, you will be able to add your dogs name later)
  • Build your dogs profile! Let other owners know if you are looking for Puppy Love or just Playmates. You will be asked whether your dogs has been spade/neutered
  • Upload photos of your dog so other users know who to look out for in the park
  • Add your next walk details so if other users are in your area they can coincide their dogs playtime
  • Start looking for playmates and start liking profiles so they appear in the dogs you admire section of your profile
  • Add comments to your own and other users profile. You can make these private so only you and the profile you are on can see them
  • If you are ever worried about another user you can report them using the link at the bottom of the webpage
